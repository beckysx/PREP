Importance of Data      link

The data I found is a collection of all the Oscar Best Picture Winners from 1927 to 2014. The data includes

  • name - Name of the movie
  • year - Year of Release of the movie
  • nomination - Number of nominations received by the movie
  • rating - User's Rating
  • duration - Duration of the movie in minutes
  • genre1 - First Genre of the movie
  • genre2 - Second Genre of the movie
  • release - Month of release of the movie
  • metacritic - Meta-Critic Rating (MCR) of the movie
  • synopsis - Synopsis of the plot of the movie

The Academy Award for Best Picture is presented annually by AMPAS and is the final award of the night and is considered the most prestigious honor of the ceremony. This media award has long history and is the world’s most influential film awards. With these data, we can see the trend of how movie changes and how people’s appetite change.
