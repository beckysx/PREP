Map Mistakes

Size of the Proportional Symbol

When the proportional symbol is circle, a mistake that people often make is they will double the radius of the circle when value doubled.

The right thing to do is double the circle's area.

Overlaps in proportinal symbol maps

Areas may get so crowded when too many overlaps happens.

The right thing to do is scale all the symbols down, make sure small symbols are infront of bigger symbols, and make all symbols semi-transparent.
